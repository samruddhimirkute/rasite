GET HELP

Occurence of inflammations, swollen and painful stiff joints (particularly early in the morning on waking) might be a sign for seeking medical advice or doctor’s help. Along with this, if eyes, mouth, skin may also show hints to seek medical advice.

Redness, pain, sensitivity to light and inflammations in eyes, dryness in mouth may indicated signs of RA.

Rheumatoid nodules (small lumps under the skin over bony areas), blood test showing results of lower than normal number of RBCs , inflammation of blood vessels and shortness of breath and lung diseases indicates strong sign of doctor’s opinion.
